Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our healthcare services.

You can schedule an appointment in several ways:

  • Through our website by logging into your account and using the appointment booking feature
  • By calling our appointment hotline at (555) 123-4567
  • By visiting our facility in person

For urgent matters, we also offer same-day appointments based on availability.

We accept most major insurance plans, including:

  • Blue Cross Blue Shield
  • Aetna
  • Cigna
  • UnitedHealthcare
  • Medicare and Medicaid

Please contact our billing department at (555) 987-6543 for specific questions about your insurance coverage.

You can access your medical records through our secure patient portal. After registering for an account and verifying your identity, you'll have access to:

  • Test results
  • Medication lists
  • Visit summaries
  • Immunization records
  • Billing information

If you need assistance accessing your records, please contact our medical records department at (555) 234-5678.

For your first appointment, please bring:

  • Valid photo ID (driver's license, passport, etc.)
  • Insurance card
  • List of current medications
  • Medical history information
  • Any relevant medical records or test results
  • Payment method for copays or deductibles

We recommend arriving 15 minutes early to complete any necessary paperwork.

Yes, we offer comprehensive telehealth services for many types of appointments. Virtual visits are available for:

  • Follow-up appointments
  • Medication management
  • Minor illness treatment
  • Mental health services
  • Chronic disease management

To schedule a telehealth appointment, use our online booking system or call our appointment line.

Our regular operating hours are:

  • Monday - Friday: 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM
  • Saturday: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
  • Sunday: Closed

Our urgent care facility is open 7 days a week from 7:00 AM - 10:00 PM.
